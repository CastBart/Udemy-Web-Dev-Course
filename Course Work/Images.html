The apartment that my girlfriend Ciara and I are currently living in is absolutley amazing. We didn't expect it to be so nice. The pictures online dont do it justice. This leads into a kitchen with a small dining table and everything a kitchen could have except an oven :( There is also a bathroom with a shower.

The city that we are staying in is La Spezia. It is a costal city and we have about 20 minute walk to the port. The food over here is amazing, you can eat all pizza and pasta you can imagine.
